Company Overview

Established in 1998, Reddy Drugs Laboratories is a leading pharmaceutical company specializing in the development and marketing of innovative animal healthcare products.

Our products cater to aquaculture, poultry, and various animal species, ensuring genuine, effective, and safe solutions.

Commitment to Quality

Reddy Drugs Laboratories adheres to GMP standards and is staffed by technically qualified professionals dedicated to maintaining consistent quality.

Quality processes are integral to our management's commitment and are upheld by all team members.

Market Presence

With a strong foothold across India, we offer a comprehensive range of practical products to meet diverse animal healthcare needs.

Our success is driven by consistent quality performance and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Innovation and Research

Recognizing the importance of innovation, we continuously invest in research and development.

Our R&D wing boasts sophisticated facilities for analytical, biological, and microbiology research, fostering creativity and new product development.

Clinical Research Focus

Clinical research is a cornerstone of our R&D efforts, leading to the production of innovative, high-quality products.

We are dedicated to discovering and developing novel formulations and drugs to improve animal healthcare.

Global Reach and Customer Service

Reddy Drugs Laboratories extends its unparalleled customer service globally, with facilities in India, Bangladesh, and Nigeria.

Our strong technical and marketing team builds lasting relationships with customers, offering value-added products and excellent service.
